Monitoring & Managing ElasticSearch with ElasticHQ

Exhaustive list of all monitored metrics and management commands available in ElasticHQ whether by data view or live self-refreshing chart. ElasticHQ enables you to manage and monitor your clusters, indices, mappings, search indices, and even make REST requests through an attractive user-interface while viewing real-time data feeds from your ElasticSearch clusters.
